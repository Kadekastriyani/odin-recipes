Soft Ice Cream

Description

Soft ice cream with simple ingredients, no fuss. Recipe by cookpad.

Ingredients

  • 5 1/2 tbsp (80 g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 10 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp (40 g) powdered milk (dancow)
  • 500 ml water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp SP

Steps

  1. Mix sweetened condensed milk, sugar, powdered milk, water and salt in a container, then cook the ingredients.
  2. Stir the cornstarch with 1/2 cup warm water, then add it to the ingredients that are being cooked when it boils, cook for another minute then let cool.
  3. Once frozen, take it out of the freezer, put it in a container, then add SP, then mix until fluffy.
  4. Give it any flavor according to taste (I use 1 sachet of pop ice for strawberry flavor, and 1 sachet of vanilla blue, for chocolate flavor I use 2 sachets of powdered chocolate).
  5. After adding the flavors, mix briefly until the flavors are evenly mixed.
  6. Put it back in the freezer until frozen (around 1 night)
