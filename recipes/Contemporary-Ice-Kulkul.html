Contemporary Ice Kulkul

Description

If you want to make modern ice kulkul that is fail-proof, you can follow this recipe. Recipe by cookpad.

Ingredients

The main ingredient:

  • 2 Bananas
  • 6 grapes
  • 6 longan seeds
  • 1 apple

Dyeing agent:

  • 50 gr dcc collata
  • 35 gr vegetable oil
  • 2 spoons of refined sugar
  • Topping according to taste
  • Ice cream skewers or skewers

Steps

  1. Peel the fruit then clean and cut according to taste.
  2. Pierce with a skewer, adjust the contents.
  3. Place in the freezer for at least 3 hours. (I've been up all night).
  4. Prepare the dip: mix, oil, dcc and powdered sugar then heat one team over hot water while stirring.
  5. Once everything is combined, transfer it to a tall glass then wait for room temperature.
  6. Take the fruit to the freezer, then dip it in the chocolate dip and sprinkle with toppings if any.
  7. Wait for two to three minutes then it's ready to eat.

Tips

Enjoy chocolate banana kulkul ice while it's cold.
