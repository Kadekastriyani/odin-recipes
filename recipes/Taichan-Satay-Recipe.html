Taichan Satay

Description

Time passes but sate taichan still never dies. The recipe is easy and the ingredients are not difficult to find. Come on, follow how to make it below! Recipe by cookpad.

Ingredients

Material:

  • 600 g chicken breast fillet, cut into 1.5 cm squares
  • 6 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 4 lime fruit, take the juice
  • 2 tsp Royco Chicken Broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
  • 3 tbsp oil

Chili ingredients:

  • 12 red cayenne pepper, boiled
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 limes, take the juice

Steps

  1. Coat the chicken evenly with Royco Chicken Broth, garlic, pepper and lime. Leave in the refrigerator for one hour.
  2. Thread 3-4 pieces of chicken onto satai skewers.
  3. Heat a roasting pan, grease with oil. Grill the satai on a grill pan, turning occasionally, until cooked. Remove, set aside.
  4. Chili: Grind the chilies and salt until smooth. Drizzle with lime juice, stir well.
  5. Serve satai with complementary chili sauce.
